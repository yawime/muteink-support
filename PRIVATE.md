## 2. 我们收集哪些信息

本 App **不要求注册账号**，也 **不会** 将您的手写笔记内容上传至开发者运营的服务器。

**在您使用本 App 时，信息主要保留在您的设备上，包括：**

- **您主动书写的内容**：笔迹、分字/分句结果、回看与导出所依据的本地数据等；  
- **与书写相关的偏好或状态**（若有）：例如与界面或会话相关的本地设置，均存储于设备。

**系统权限：**

- **照片图库（相册）**：仅在您使用 **将动图（GIF）保存到相册** 或 **从相册选取/写入** 等相关功能时，按系统提示申请；我们不会访问您相册中与使用本 App 无关的其他内容。用途与系统弹窗说明一致：**用于将您书写的内容与特效导出为动图并保存到相册，便于分享**。

本 App **当前设计为以本机存储为主**；除上述及系统提供的能力外，**不** 将您的笔记内容用于广告画像或出售给第三方。

## 3. 数据存储与安全

- 笔记与相关数据默认保存在您设备上的 **应用沙盒**（如系统「应用支持」目录下的本地文件）中，由 **您设备的系统安全机制**（如锁屏、系统备份策略等）保护。  
- **卸载本 App** 可能导致上述本地数据被系统删除；请在卸载前自行备份重要内容（例如通过导出 GIF、系统备份等方式）。

# Privacy Policy (MuteInk / 默记)

**Last updated: April 2026**

This policy describes how the mobile app **MuteInk** (「默记」, “the App”) handles information when you use it. By using the App, you agree to this policy.

## Operator & contact

- **Developer**: Weiming Yang  
- **Support**: [muteink-support](https://github.com/yawime/muteink-support)  
- **Feedback**: [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/yawime/muteink-support/issues)  

## What we collect

The App **does not require an account** and **does not upload your handwriting notes** to servers operated by the developer for core note-taking.

**Information stays primarily on your device**, including:

- Content you write (strokes, character/sentence structure used for review and export);  
- Local preferences or session state related to writing (if any).

**Permissions:**

- **Photo Library**: Requested only when you **save a GIF to Photos** or use related features that require Photos access, per the system prompt. We do not use unrelated photos. The stated purpose matches the App’s usage descriptions: **to save exported animations of your writing for sharing**.

## Storage & security

Data is stored in the App’s **sandbox** on your device and protected by **iOS/iPadOS** security. **Deleting the App** may erase local data; back up anything important before uninstalling.

## Third parties

Core features do not rely on a developer-run cloud account to store your notes. If we add analytics, crash reporting, or cloud sync in the future, we will update this policy and the App’s disclosures and obtain consent or permissions where required.

## Children

The App is not specifically directed to children. Minors should use it with guardian guidance.

## Your choices

Manage notes and local data in the App; manage **Photos** and other permissions in **Settings**. Revoking permissions may disable some features.

## Changes

We may update this policy; the **Last updated** date will change. Material changes may be highlighted in the App or store release notes.

## Governing law

To the extent permitted by mandatory local laws, local law may apply to interpretation and disputes. Contact us via the channels above.
